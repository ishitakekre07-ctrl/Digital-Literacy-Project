✅ Do’s (Things you should do)
✔️ Think before you post – once something is online, it can stay there forever
✔️ Keep your accounts secure by using strong passwords and extra security (like OTP login)
✔️ Double-check information before sharing so you don’t spread fake news
✔️ Use privacy settings wisely to control who can see your posts and personal details
✔️ Be respectful and kind while commenting or messaging others
❌ Don’ts (Things to avoid)
❌ Don’t share sensitive details like passwords, OTPs, or bank information
❌ Don’t post in anger or frustration — it can harm your image and relationships
❌ Don’t accept requests from strangers without knowing who they are
❌ Don’t click on random or suspicious links — they can be scams
❌ Don’t overshare your life, like your location or daily routine, for safety reasons
