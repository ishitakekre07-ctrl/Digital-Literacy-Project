This infographic explains the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students in today’s digital world. It defines digital literacy as the ability to use technology effectively and responsibly to access, evaluate, create, and share information. The visual also highlights useful digital tools such as Google Workspace, note-taking apps, and task managers that support learning. In addition, it emphasizes safe internet practices like keeping strong passwords, thinking before clicking, and protecting personal privacy. The design combines clear sections and visuals to make learning engaging and easy to understand. Overall, it encourages students to be smart, responsible, and safe while using digital technologies.
