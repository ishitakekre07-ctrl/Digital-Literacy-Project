✔️ Do’s
-Think before posting — make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.
-Protect your privacy — use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.
-Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
-Be respectful and kind in comments and messages.
-Use social media productively — for learning, networking, and skill-building.
❌ Don’ts
-Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, etc.).
-Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling.
-Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content that could harm your reputation.
-Don’t believe or spread fake news without checking facts.
-Don’t spend excessive time on social media that affects your studies or health.
